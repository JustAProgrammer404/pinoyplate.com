Tatak Pinoy

Pork Barbeque

Sweet and savory Filipino-style pork barbecue on skewers.

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Soy Sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Ketchup
  • Calamansi
  • Sugar
  • Garlic
  • Pepper
  • Skewers

Cooking Procedure

  1. Slice pork and marinate.
  2. Skewer pieces.
  3. Grill while brushing with marinade.
