Tatak Pinoy

Kwek-Kwek

Deep-fried quail eggs coated in orange batter, served with spiced vinegar.

Ingredients

  • Quail eggs
  • Flour
  • Cornstarch
  • Water
  • Annatto Powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Cooking Procedure

  1. Boil and peel quail eggs.
  2. Prepare orange batter.
  3. Coat eggs and deep-fry.
