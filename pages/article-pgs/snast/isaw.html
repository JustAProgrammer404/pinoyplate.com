Tatak Pinoy

Isaw (Bitukang Manok)

Grilled marinated chicken intestines, a popular Filipino street barbecue.

Ingredients

  • Chicken intestines
  • Vinegar
  • Garlic < /li>
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Soy Sauce
  • Oil
  • Orange Food Color

Cooking Procedure

  1. Clean intestines well.
  2. Boil with vinegar and garlic.
  3. Add the soy sauce
  4. Add orange food color.
  5. Skewer and marinate.
  6. Grill until slightly charred.
