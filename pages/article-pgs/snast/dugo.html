Tatak Pinoy

Dugo (Betamax)

Grilled coagulated pork or chicken blood cut into square shapes.

Ingredients

  • Pork/chicken blood
  • Vinegar
  • Soy Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Pepper
  • Skewers

Cooking Procedure

  1. Cut solidified blood into cubes.
  2. Marinate in soy sauce and vinegar.
  3. Skewer and grill.
