Tatak Pinoy

Bibingka

A classic Filipino rice cake baked with coconut milk, butter, and salted egg.

Ingredients

  • Rice flour
  • Coconut Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Salted Egg
  • Banana Leaf

Cooking Procedure

  1. Prepare the batter.
  2. Line mold with banana leaf.
  3. Pour batter and add salted egg.
  4. Bake until golden.
