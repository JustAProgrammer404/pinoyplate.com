Tatak Pinoy

Lumpiang Shanghai

Filipino-style fried spring rolls filled with ground pork, carrots, and seasonings.

Ingredients

  • Ground pork
  • Carrots
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Spring Roll Wrappers
  • Egg
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oil

Cooking Procedure

  1. Mix pork, vegetables, and seasonings.
  2. Wrap into rolls.
  3. Fry until golden brown.
