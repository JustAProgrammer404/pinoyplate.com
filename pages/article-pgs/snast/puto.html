Tatak Pinoy

Puto

Soft and fluffy Filipino steamed rice cakes often topped with cheese.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Cheese

Cooking Procedure

  1. Mix dry ingredients.
  2. Add wet ingredients and blend.
  3. Pour into molds.
  4. Steam until fluffy.
  5. Add cheese on top.
