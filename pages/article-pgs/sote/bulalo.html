Tatak Pinoy

Bulalo

A light beef shank and bone marrow soup served with vegetables and corn.

Ingredients

  • Beef shank with bone marrow
  • Onion
  • Peppercorns
  • Corn
  • Cabbage
  • Pechay
  • Fish sauce
  • Water

Cooking Procedure

  1. Boil beef shanks until tender, remove scum.
  2. Add onion and peppercorns.
  3. Add corn and simmer.
  4. Add vegetables.
  5. Season with fish sauce.
  6. Serve hot.
