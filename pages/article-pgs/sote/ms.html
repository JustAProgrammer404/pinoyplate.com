Tatak Pinoy

Molo Soup

A Filipino dumpling soup from Iloilo, made with pork-filled wontons and chicken broth.

Ingredients

  • Ground pork
  • Molo (wonton) wrappers
  • Garlic & onion
  • Chicken broth
  • Shredded chicken
  • Salt & pepper
  • Spring onions
  • Fried garlic

Cooking Procedure

  1. Sauté garlic and onion.
  2. Add shredded chicken and broth.
  3. Prepare pork-filled molo wrappers.
  4. Add dumplings to simmering broth.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Top with green onions and garlic.
