Tatak Pinoy

Pares (with rice)

A sweet-savory beef stew paired with garlic fried rice and clear beef soup.

Ingredients

  • Beef brisket
  • Garlic & onion
  • Soy sauce
  • Sugar
  • Star anise
  • Pepper
  • Water/broth
  • Cornstarch (optional)
  • Cooked rice
  • Extra garlic for fried rice

Cooking Procedure

  1. Sauté garlic and onion.
  2. Add beef and brown slightly.
  3. Add soy sauce, sugar, star anise, water.
  4. Simmer until tender.
  5. Thicken with cornstarch slurry if desired.
  6. Make garlic fried rice separately.
  7. Serve with rice and beef soup.
