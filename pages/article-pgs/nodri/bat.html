Tatak Pinoy

Batchoy

A rich Iloilo noodle soup with miki noodles, pork, broth, liver, and chicharon.

Ingredients

  • Miki noodles
  • Pork bones and beef bones
  • Pork meat and liver
  • Garlic & onion
  • Fish sauce
  • Pepper
  • Chicharon
  • Spring onions

Cooking Procedure

  1. Simmer pork and beef bones to make broth.
  2. Sauté garlic and onion, add to broth.
  3. Add pork meat and liver; cook until tender.
  4. Season with fish sauce and pepper.
  5. Blanch miki noodles.
  6. Assemble noodles, pour broth, add toppings.
