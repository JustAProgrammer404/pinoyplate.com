Tatak Pinoy

Pancit Canton

A Filipino stir-fried noodle dish made with wheat (canton) noodles, assorted vegetables (carrot, cabbage, sometimes green beans), slices of meat (chicken, pork or shrimp) and flavored with soy sauce and/or oyster sauce.

Ingredients

  • Pancit canton noodles
  • Chicken or pork (sliced)
  • Shrimp (optional)
  • Garlic & onion
  • Carrots, cabbage, green beans
  • Soy sauce
  • Oyster sauce
  • Chicken broth or water
  • Salt & pepper
  • Oil

Cooking Procedure

  1. Soak or rinse the canton noodles as needed, set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a large pan or wok; sauté minced garlic and chopped onion until fragrant.
  3. Add meat (e.g., chicken/pork) and cook until lightly browned. Add shrimp if using.
  4. Add vegetables (carrots, cabbage, etc.), stir-fry for a minute or two.
  5. Push ingredients to one side, add the noodles, pour a bit of broth (or water) and soy/oyster sauce; toss so noodles cook and absorb flavor, combine with veggies and meat.
  6. Adjust seasoning (salt/pepper/sauce) and cook until the noodles are done but still springy.
  7. Serve hot, garnish optionally with sliced green onions or calamansi.
