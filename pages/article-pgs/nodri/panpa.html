Tatak Pinoy

Pancit Palabok

A classic Filipino rice-noodle dish topped with a rich orange shrimp-flavored sauce, ground pork, tinapa flakes, hard-boiled eggs, chicharrón, spring onions, and calamansi. Famous for its savory seafood taste and festive toppings.

Ingredients

  • Bihon noodles (rice noodles)
  • Ground pork
  • Shrimp stock
  • Annatto (atsuete)
  • Garlic & onion
  • Cornstarch
  • Tinapa flakes
  • Hard-boiled eggs
  • Cooked shrimp
  • Chicharrón
  • Spring onions
  • Fish sauce

Cooking Procedure

  1. Cook noodles and set aside.
  2. Sauté garlic and onion; add ground pork.
  3. Add shrimp stock and annatto water.
  4. Thicken with cornstarch slurry.
  5. Season with fish sauce.
  6. Pour sauce over noodles and add toppings.
  7. Serve with calamansi.
