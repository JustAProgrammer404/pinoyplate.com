Ginataang Tilapia

Tilapia cooked in coconut milk with vegetables, garlic, onion, and chili for a creamy, flavorful dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 tilapia, cleaned and sliced
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 chili, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetables (squash, string beans, eggplant)

Cooking Procedure

  1. Fry the fish until golden brown.
  2. Sauté garlic, onion, ginger and chili.
  3. Add coconut milk and bring to a boil.
  4. Add tilapia and vegetables. Simmer until cooked.
  5. Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot.
