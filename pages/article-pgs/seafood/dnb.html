Daing na Bangus

Milkfish marinated in vinegar, garlic, and pepper, then fried to crispy perfection.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole bangus, butterflied
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 5 cloves garlic, crushed
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying

Cooking Procedure

  1. Marinate bangus in vinegar, garlic, salt, and pepper for at least 1 hour.
  2. Heat oil in pan and fry until golden brown on both sides.
  3. Serve with rice and dipping sauce.
