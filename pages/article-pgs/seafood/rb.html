Rellenong Bangus

Deboned milkfish stuffed with savory fillings like vegetables, ground pork, and spices, baked or fried to perfection.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole bangus (milkfish), deboned
  • 100g ground pork
  • 1 carrot, finely chopped
  • 1/2 onion, minced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Cooking Procedure

  1. Sauté garlic, onion, carrot, and ground pork. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Stuff the mixture into the deboned bangus.
  3. Secure with toothpicks and brush with oil.
  4. Bake at 180°C for 30 minutes or until golden brown.
