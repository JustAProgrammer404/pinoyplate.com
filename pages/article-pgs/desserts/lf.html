Tatak Pinoy

Leche Flan

A rich Filipino custard dessert made of egg yolks, milk (often condensed or evaporated), and caramelized sugar. A staple at celebrations.

Ingredients

  • 10 egg yolks
  • 1 can (370 ml) condensed milk
  • 1 can (370 ml) evaporated milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • 1 cup sugar (for caramel)

Cooking Procedure

  1. Separate egg yolks (use only yolks). Beat gently – avoid creating too much foam.
  2. Add condensed milk and fresh/evaporated milk, and vanilla extract if using. Mix gently.
  3. In a flan mold (llanera) or baking pan, caramelize sugar over low heat until golden‑brown. Swirl to coat the bottom and sides.
  4. Pour the egg‑milk mixture into the mold with the caramel. Cover mold with foil.
  5. Steam (or bake in a water bath) until set (about ~30–45 mins depending on size).
  6. Cool, refrigerate, then invert onto a plate so caramel coats the custard. Serve chilled.
