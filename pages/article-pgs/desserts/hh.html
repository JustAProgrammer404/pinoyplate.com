Tatak Pinoy

Halo‑Halo

The iconic Filipino shaved‑ice dessert. A mix (halo = mix) of sweet beans, jellies, fruits, shaved ice and milk, topped with leche flan, ube halaya or ice‑cream. Served in a tall glass and stirred before eating.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup sweetened red beans
  • 1/4 cup sweetened white beans
  • 1/4 cup macapuno (sweetened coconut strips)
  • 1/4 cup kaong (sugar palm fruit)
  • 1/4 cup nata de coco
  • 1/4 cup jackfruit, sliced
  • Crushed or shaved ice
  • 1/4 cup evaporated or condensed milk
  • 1 scoop ube halaya or ice cream
  • 1 slice leche flan (optional)

Cooking Procedure

  1. In a tall glass, layer sweet ingredients: sweetened beans, macapuno coconut strips, kaong, nata de coco, jackfruit, etc.
  2. Add a generous amount of crushed or shaved ice.
  3. Pour evaporated milk or sweetened condensed milk over the ice.
  4. Top with a scoop of ube (purple yam) halaya or ice cream, and a slice of leche flan if desired.
  5. Serve with a long spoon and straw; stir everything together thoroughly before eating.
