Tatak Pinoy

Tortang Talong

A Filipino eggplant omelette: grilled or broiled eggplant whose skin is peeled, then dipped in beaten egg (and sometimes ground meat is added) and fried until crispy.

Ingredients

  • 3–4 medium eggplants
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 100 g ground pork or beef (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooking oil

Cooking Procedure

  1. Grill or roast eggplant until skin is charred and flesh is soft; peel the skin leaving the stem.
  2. Flatten the eggplant gently.
  3. Dip each piece in beaten egg (seasoned with salt & pepper), optionally top with ground meat or cheese.
  4. Fry in oil until golden and cooked through. Drain and serve with rice and banana‑ketchup or vinegar dip.
