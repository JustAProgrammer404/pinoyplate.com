Ginataang Kalabasa at Sitaw

A vegetable dish cooked in coconut milk (gata) featuring squash (kalabasa) and long beans (sitaw). The “ginataang” style means “in coconut milk”.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cubed squash (kalabasa)
  • 1 cup long beans (sitaw), cut into 2‑inch pieces
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 100 g shrimp or pork (optional)
  • Salt or fish sauce to taste
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil

Cooking Procedure

  1. Sauté garlic, onion, and optional protein (shrimp/pork) if used.
  2. Add coconut milk; bring to light boil.
  3. Add cubed squash; cook until beginning to soften.
  4. Add long beans sitaw; continue cooking until vegetables are tender.
  5. Season with fish sauce or salt, and add more coconut cream if a richer sauce is desired. Serve with rice.
