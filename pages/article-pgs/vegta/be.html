Tatak Pinoy

Bicol Express

A richly spiced dish of pork cooked in coconut milk with chilis and shrimp paste. Creamy and hot, it’s a Filipino favorite.

Ingredients

  • 500 g pork belly, cubed
  • 2–3 cups coconut milk
  • 2–3 chili peppers (labuyo or Thai chili)
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1–2 tbsp shrimp paste (bagoong)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil

Cooking Procedure

  1. Sauté garlic, onion, and ginger in oil. Add pork belly and lightly brown.
  2. Add shrimp paste (bagoong) and stir.
  3. Pour in coconut milk, add chilis (Thai or bird’s eye), and simmer until pork is tender and sauce has reduced.
  4. Add coconut cream near the end for richness; continue simmering until thickened.
  5. Season to taste and serve hot with steamed rice.
